How it works

  1. 1

    Verify your identity

    Authenticate with LinkedIn or generate a zero-knowledge proof via Self.xyz. No personal data stored.

  2. 2

    Claim your .cv domain

    Your name becomes your domain. johndoe.cv is yours — free for your first year. Registration completes in about 30 minutes.

  3. 3

    Registry anchors the proof

    We write an immutable cv-verify TXT record to your domain's DNS. You can't fake it. Nobody can alter it.

  4. 4

    Agents inherit trust

    Every subdomain — assistant.johndoe.cv, research.johndoe.cv — traces back to you.

For developers & agents

            

              

                
Register & verify via terminal

                
# Install the id.cv CLI
npm install -g @id/cv

# Register your domain
idcv register --name "John Doe"

# Verify via LinkedIn
idcv verify --method linkedin

# Verify via Self.xyz
idcv verify --method self

# Check verification status
idcv status johndoe.cv

# Lookup any .cv domain
dig +short TXT johndoe.cv

                
Works in any terminal, IDE, or agent environment.

              

              

                
Use in your agent / MCP server

                
# Verify an agent's identity
dig +short TXT agent.johndoe.cv

# Single domain lookup
